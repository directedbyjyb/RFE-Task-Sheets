Welcome to the RFE Shift Task Viewer! Here's how to navigate:

  • Click on the collapsible tabs to expand the documents.
  • Use the Hamburger Menu in the top-right corner for additional options such as submitting feedback regarding task sheets.
  • Click "Go Back to Selection" to modify your shift selection.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact the admin team.
